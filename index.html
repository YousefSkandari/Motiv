Get Started

What is MOTiV?

MOTiV is an app that allows anyone to plan their events with friends, family, work colleagues or whoever they wish. Giving all event goers the ability to know how long each participant is going to take before arriving at the event and being able to maintain constant communication and connection together. Events that consist of large group may benefit greatly from MOTiV as group payments can be completed hassle free.

How MOTiV Will Work

Find Local Motives Near You

MOTiV will have all the local resturants, leasure and monumental sites available for you and your friends to organise. MOTIV aims to recommend the most ideal places for you based on your previous searches and geo-location information, this makes picking a place out for a date far easier.

See How Long It Will Take For Your Friends To Arrive

We keep track of your friends for you so you don't have to worry. It's always better to know how long your "always late" friend John Doe is going to be. Calling him every 30 minutes to see where they are when they are late is only going to get more annoying, so it helps to know where John is.

Time To Pay

Users have the ability to ‘split the bill’ or even put money towards a surprise birthday gift. The shared wallet is to make any sort of transactions seamless with more than 2 people making the MOTiV less of a drag when it ends. Users will upload their card details to a MOTiV wallet, once everyone's card details are uploaded the app will work out how to split the bill between the attendees.

Getting All The Pictures

We all get bombarded with pictures and videos from the last event from dozens of different social messaging apps, so why not just leave it in one place where we can all post, share and talk about each piece of media conveniently? Its as easy as clicking the upload button and selecting all the images and videos from your gallery and posting it in the event you previously had.

Our Ethos

MOTiV wants to disrupt the digital industry. We believe that user want to trust the products the they are using, so in response to that our aim is to be as close to 100% transparent as possible. We want to share what data we will be using/sharing along with who we share that data with, also we want to give users the clear option to opt out their data being used/shared in a clear informative way. To gain trust as well as a positive relationship with our users we believe that the data we store is theirs, that therefore means that they should be earning some sort of revenue from their data along with information as to where it goes.
